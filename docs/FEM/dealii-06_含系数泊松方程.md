# step-5, Poisson equation with coefficient

$$
-\nabla \cdot a(x) \nabla u(x) = 1 \quad \text{in } \Omega,
$$

$$
u = 0 \quad \text{on } \partial \Omega.
$$

$$
a(x) =
\begin{cases}
20 & \text{if } |x| < 0.5, \\
1 & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}
$$
